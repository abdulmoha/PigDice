Pig-Dice

About Pig-dice

Pig is a simple dice game.It is played with many rule variations. Pig is most commonly used by mathematics teachers to teach probability concepts.

Rules

These are the rules for the pig-dice game:

  • If a player rolls a 1,they get nothing and the turn goes to the other player.
  • If a player rolls any other number,it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
